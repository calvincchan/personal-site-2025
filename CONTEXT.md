# CONTEXT.md — Domain Glossary

Terms used in this codebase and its content strategy. This file is a glossary only — no implementation details.

---

## AI-Fluent Workflow

Using AI tools (local LLMs, RAG pipelines, MCP, agents, code agents) as productivity multipliers, directed by engineering discipline and experience. Not "vibe coding" — Calvin guides AI with architectural judgment and verifies output. Preferred term: **AI-Fluent**, not AI-native or AI-assisted.

---

## Engagement Model

Calvin's preferred work arrangement: **fractional/ongoing contract**. Not full-time employment. Not fixed-scope one-offs (though acceptable). Ideal engagement = part-time or project-length embed on a team, weeks to months, with full-lifecycle ownership.

---

## Full-Lifecycle Ownership

The ability to own a system from architecture through implementation, deployment, and ongoing maintenance — including replacing legacy systems and designing multi-layer features end-to-end. Distinguishes Calvin from feature-only contractors.

---

## Proof Post

A blog post structured as evidence of competence: problem → what was built → outcome / lesson. Contrast with **Learning Log** (personal notes written for the author, not for a client audience).

---

## Target Client

SaaS founders and SMBs who need a senior full-stack developer with AI fluency. Not enterprise. Not a specific vertical (maritime, health, eCommerce are past clients, not the target positioning).

---

## Tech Signature

Calvin's core stack: TypeScript, Node.js, React, Supabase, Next.js. Willingness and track record of adapting to other stacks when client need requires it.
